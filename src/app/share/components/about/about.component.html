Welcome to the Quiz App!

This application is designed to provide an interactive and engaging quiz experience. You can test your knowledge on various topics and challenge yourself.

Key Features:
  • Variety of quizzes on different subjects
  • User-friendly interface
  • Instant feedback on your answers

How to Use:

Simply select a quiz from the available list and start answering the questions. Your score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.
